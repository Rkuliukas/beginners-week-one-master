What is Chocolate?

Chocolate is a tasty brown treat which comes in the form of a block, but can be melted into a thick liquid. Chocolate can be consumed by itself or added to any food/reciepe as a delicous flavouring ingredient, such as Cake and Ice Cream.

How is Chocolate made?

Chocolate is made with these ingredients:

  • Cocoa bean
  • Milk or alternatiive
  • Sugar
